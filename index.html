Evolvra — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Evolvra ("we", "our", "us") is a personal development and habit tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and profile photo (if provided). You may also sign in using Google or Apple, in which case we receive basic profile information from these providers. You can also use the app as a guest without providing personal information.

User-Generated Content: We store the tasks, habits, goals, and progress data you create within the app, including completion status, streaks, XP points, levels, and badges.

Social Features: If you use social features, we store your friend connections, chat messages, challenge participation, and leaderboard rankings.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics such as feature usage events, screen views, and app performance data to improve the app experience.

Device Information: We collect push notification tokens to send you reminders and notifications. We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.

Purchase Information: If you subscribe to Evolvra Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (task tracking, habit management, progress tracking)
  • To enable social features (friends, chat, challenges, leaderboards)
  • To send push notifications and daily reminders (when enabled by you)
  • To process and manage your Pro subscription
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage analytics
  • To provide AI-powered task suggestions (Pro feature)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Storage). All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, data storage, analytics, and push notifications
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: For payment processing

These service providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile in the app
  • Update or correct your information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Use the app as a guest without providing personal information

6. Children's Privacy

Evolvra is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
